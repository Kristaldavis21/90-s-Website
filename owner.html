Kristal Davis Mitchell, M.B.A

Was born and raised in Southampton, Bermuda. She attended the Art Institue of Atlanta were she obtained a BFA in Fashion & Retail Management. Upon completion she moved to West, New York New Jersey and attended LIM College in midtown Manhattan. In July 2013 she graduated with a Masters of Business Administration with a focus on Business Management & Entrepreneurship. Kristal has always had a passion for customer service and getting the job done. She has been employed in several positions that have allowed her to excel professionally and to continue to grow her skillsets. Currently Kristal is enrolled in The Digital Crafts Web Development Bootcamp through the partnership of The City of Refugee and The Georgia Department of Labor.
