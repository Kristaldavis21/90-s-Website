FAQ

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to check-in (by 3:00 PM PST). Cancellations made less than 24 hours prior to check-in will result in a credit card charge for one night. Special cancellation policies may apply for packages, promotions, certain arrival dates, and rates.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check in time is at 3:00 PM and check out is at 11:00 AM. We cannot guarantee either early or late check-in, but as with all special requests we will always note it in your reservation. Please note there is a half-day rate (half of best available rate) for staying in your room until 5:00 PM, and checking out after 5:00 PM warrants a full day rate (based on the best available rate of the hotel). Again, late check-out is only based on availability and with confirmation from the front desk.

How do I reserve an open-air cabana?

For open-air cabana rentals, please contact our concierge.

Is smoking allowed at the hotel?

The Sleepover Hotel has designated smoke-areas. Smoking inside rooms is prohitbited.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have enclosed onsite parking with in and out privileges. This is included in our resort fee.
